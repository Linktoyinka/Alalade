The Brief

About Kweek

“Kweek” is a smartphone application designed to solve some common logistics problems encountered by users.

Objective

Design a mobile app that helps users find logistics companies that best meet their needs based on factors such as price, availability and ratings.



Checkout the prototypes

Understanding the users

Research Summary

I conducted foundational research to understand the logistics industry. After getting basic understanding of the industry, I conducted semi-structured interviews with 5 users. The interviewees were selected from many people who filled out a screener survey, because they matched the target demography identified from foundational research.

User Persona

I created a user persona which is representative of Plubo’s primary user group based on data from the user research.

Cynthia Jacobs.

Occupation
University Student
Location
New York, USA
Age
22
Family
Lives with parents and a sibling

Problem Statement

Cynthia is a university student who needs to find and purchase trendy fashion items because they want to impress people in their social circle.

Biography

Cynthia is a media analyst at a branding agency in Ibadan, Nigeria. After working hours, she runs her business (selling cosmetics products) from home. Most of her clients do not live in Ibadan, so she is often left with no choice but to send her goods to them through third-parties, which she finds frustrating because of poor parcel handling, timing issues, and parcel tracking difficulties.

Goals

  • To increase business returns.
  • To have more time for other activities.
  • To make business fulfilment faster and less stressful.

Frustrations

  • “I do not enjoy having to call over and over to track a parcel.”
  • “It is a hassle getting inexpensive but trustworthy companies.”
  • “Logistics fulfilment personnel are often lackadaisical about my parcels.”

Pain Points

The insights we have gathered can be summarized into three main pain points.

Communication
Logistic fulfilment processes involve too many calls.
Company Verification
Users find it difficult to find and verify companies safety.
Price Comparison
Comparing logistic company prices is a stressful activity for users.
