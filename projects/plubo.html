The Brief

About Plubo

Plubo is an online boutique retail store. Users can find and purchase trendy fashion items all within the app. The app targets users who are fashion enthusiasts.

Objective

Develop a dedicated e-commerce mobile application and a responsive website to facilitate seamless product purchases and provide users with fashion information.

Checkout the prototypes

Understanding the users

Research Summary

I conducted interviews and created empath maps to understand the users I’m designing for and their needs. The primary user group identified through the research was young and middle aged adults (18 - 45 years) who have frequent social engagements and need to update their wardrobe regularly.

User Persona

I created a user persona which is representative of Plubo’s primary user group based on data from the user research.

Tomiwa Adebayo.

Occupation
University Student
Location
New York, USA
Age
22
Family
Lives with parents and a sibling

Problem Statement

Tomiwa is a university student who needs to find and purchase trendy fashion items because they want to impress people in their social circle.

Biography

Tomiwa recently gained admission into the university, and is looking to expand their social horizon. Between attending social functions and lectures, they are confronted with a lack of fashion items for their regular needs. They also like to keep up with the latest trends.

Goals

  • Be more socially active and make connections that will be useful in the future.
  • Increase relationship appeal and find a romantic partner.
  • Appear and boost their confidence level.

Frustrations

  • “I often have to download multiple apps to keep up with trends.
  • “I would like to make purchases online, but most items are poorly described and I often end up with size and quality related issues”.
  • “I often feel ignored by the staff in the boutique, and I have to spend a long time searching for the items myself.

Pain Points

The insights we have gathered can be summarized into three main pain points.

Time
Keeping up with the latest fashion trends takes a lot of time.
Cataloguing
Platforms for purchasing fashion items often create problems for users by having poor catalogues and product sorting.
Copy
Product descriptions are usually text heavy and vague or too short.
