About Make Known

Make Known is a Canadian marketing agency that focuses on delivering affordable branding and social media solutions to small businesses. Make Known aims to empower small businesses by equipping them with effective marketing tools.

My role involved creating a website that conveys Make Known's services clearly and reflects their core values. I was also tasked with creating a visual identity system for the brand.
